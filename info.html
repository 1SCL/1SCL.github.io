What is the Shell Shockers Competitive League (SCL)
The SCL, is a competitive league for shell shockers. This website wil also provide liks for shell shockers.
You will play 10 games, and then show your games to a host in a ticket, and then you will be ranked by your preformance. There also will be tournaments so try to join those.
There will be prizes but currently we are getting ranks and more members. Only r3+ can play in tournaments, and there can only be even amount of teams for duos and squads, max for both is 24 teams.
